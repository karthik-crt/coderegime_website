Privacy Policy

At CodeRegime Technologies, our team prefers securing your details. This Privacy Policy portrays how we collect, use, and protect the data you provide when visiting or buying from our website, https://www.coderegimetech.com.

Information We Collect

We gather two types of information:

Digital Footprint: This specific details about your web browser, IP address, time zone, and browsing behavior, such as the pages you view and referral sources.

Smart Buy Information: When you make a purchase, we can get your name, billing, shipping address, email, and mobile number to complete your transaction.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected serves multiple purposes:

To process orders, arrange deliveries, and send purchase confirmations.

To improve website functionality by analyzing user interactions.

To ensure the security of our platform by monitoring for potential threats or fraud.
